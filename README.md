# Further-Study
Further Study is a web-based platform that helps students easily explore and compare universities without relying only on consultancies.

It provides a centralized system where students can access university information such as programs, tuition fees, and locations.

## Inspiration
This idea came from observing how many students depend heavily on consultancies when choosing universities. 
Often, students are influenced without fully understanding their options or preparing properly.

As an international student in Korea, I wanted to create a platform that gives students more control and awareness in their decision-making process.

## Problem
Many students face challenges when choosing universities:
- Limited knowledge about available universities
- Over-reliance on consultancies
- Risk of being misinformed
- Lack of clarity about programs
- Poor preparation before applying

## Solution
Further Study solves this by:
- Providing a platform where universities can upload program details
- Allowing students to search and explore universities globally
- Enabling comparison of tuition fees, programs, and locations
- Making all information accessible in one place

## Features
- Add university information
- Search universities
- View program details
- Compare tuition fees and locations

## How It Works
1. Universities create an account (future improvement)
2. Universities upload:
   - University name
   - Country
   - Program
   - Tuition fee
3. Students visit the website
4. Students search and compare universities
